SATYANARAYAN POOJA

In the Hindu religion, there are various ways to express devotion to God and offering prayers while performing particular rituals in one way of executing it. By Hindus, satyanarayan pooja is one of the most frequently performed pooja.
REAL MEANING OF SATYANARAYAN POOJA:
Satyanarayan Pooja is the religious worship of the Hindus god Vishnu. In real words, Satya means truth and Narayan means the highest being so the combined means of satyanarayan is the highest being who is an embodiment of trust. Hindus perform Shri satyanarayan pooja or vrat for divine blessings of wealth, health, education, prosperity, relief from sickness and trouble. This pooja is also performed because of success in business or career growth or during a social function like a house warming ceremony, naming of the children, marriage ceremony etc. Satyanarayan pooja kit consists of the pooja items like- satyanarayan photo, satyanarayan pooja book, Haldi powder, sindoor powder, gulaal powder, kumkum powder, Kapur packet, janoi, clay diyas, pooja badam, pooja Akron, pooja Haldi sticks etc.
WHO AND WHEN TO PERFORM SATYANARAYAN POOJA:
Satyanarayan pooja can be performed on any ordinary day and tithi. On hold, do not wait for any festival. Nonetheless, the Ekadashi tithi or Purnima tithi day is considered an ideal day for performing the satyanarayan pooja. Anyone irrespective of any caste, gender and age can conduct the pooja at their home and workplace.
